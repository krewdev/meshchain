Why MeshChain

Designed for off-grid hold/spend, extreme cold storage, and dual-control vaults.

1

Mesh-native money

Balances live on a lightweight ledger that hops over LoRa Meshtastic — no cell tower required to hold or send MESH.

2

Simple wallets

The mesh CLI is deliberately plain English so you can run a full service without a graphical UI.

3

Hybrid vault lock

Lock SOL/stables on Solana bound to your mesh address. Unlock needs mesh burn + witnesses — not the internet alone.

4

Quantum-safe cold path

Large and vault-linked actions use ML-DSA-65 (FIPS 204). Keys stay offline; radio can stay powered off while holding.

5

Privacy-minded

Hashed redeem destinations, optional stealth receive tags, private funds channel — with honest limits of RF privacy.

6

Open source

MIT licensed. Audit the ledger, vault program, and transport. Run your own validators and attestors.
